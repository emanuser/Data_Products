The app

  • The app is a Seismic hazard map

  • Cluster map

    • Seismic events by region and number

  • Heat map

    • Seismic events by frequancy and magnitude

  • https://emanresu.shinyapps.io/EQ_cluster/

Why it is importent

  • Assist engineers in designing buildings, bridges, and highways

  • Assist cties, counties, and local governments

  • Help establish construction requirements necessary to preserve public safety

  • Help business in land-use planning

How it was built

  • The app wasbuilt in R with the use of

    • 30 day seismic data from USGA
    • Spatial data frames
    • Leaflet
    • Rcharts
    • Leaflet heatmap plugin
